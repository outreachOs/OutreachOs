📞 Cold Call Script
👋 Opening
"Hey, is that [Name]? Hi — I'll be upfront, this is a cold call, I'll keep it quick. Is now okay?"
💡 Honesty disarms them instantly
🎯 The Hook (10 sec)
"I was searching for plumbers in [City] and your site wasn't coming up. I help local tradesmen rank higher on Google and fix their website — just thought it was worth a chat."
✅ If They Say Go On
"I had a look at your site — there are a few things stopping Google from ranking you that your competitors are already doing. I'd love to do a free 10-minute audit and show you what I found. No strings."
💡 Remove all sales pressure
🤝 The Close
"Can I send you a quick summary of what I found? Totally free. What's the best email?"
💡 Email ask is softer than booking a meeting
