About Kris Recovery

We're Not a Call Centre — We're Local

Kris Car and Van Recovery has been serving Leicester and Leicestershire for years. When you ring the number, you get Kris directly. No automated menus, no being passed around, no waiting in a queue. Just a local man who picks up the phone and gets to you fast.

As someone who has lived and worked in Leicester his whole life, Kris knows every road, estate, industrial area and shortcut in the city and surrounding areas. That local knowledge means we genuinely get to you faster than national operators working from a map.

Our reputation is built entirely on doing the job properly, charging fairly, and treating every customer the way we'd want to be treated in a stressful situation. That's why most of our work comes from repeat customers and word-of-mouth across Leicestershire.
